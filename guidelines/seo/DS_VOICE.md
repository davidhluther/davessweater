## Voice (as already stated in CLAUDE.md)

- **Dry, wry, factual, having fun — sharp but never bitter.** "Boone's #2 weather
  resource."
- Throughline: *"Every forecast is a claim about tomorrow. We check them all —
  including ours."*
- Deeper frame: **data democracy** — public data (satellites, models, stations)
  made useful and free, versus access sold back behind a paywall and a résumé.
  Ray's Weather is the named symbol of gated expertise: pointed at, never bitter,
  and always credited where the data credits him.
- Credibility rests on the tracker being real and the methodology visible and
  defensible. **Frame claims as tracked data, not assertion.**

## Sourcing discipline (from spec §7)

- Every stat traceable — for DS that usually means **our own dataset**
  (`data/scores.json` and friends). Blog posts always link the scoreboard and
  methodology.
